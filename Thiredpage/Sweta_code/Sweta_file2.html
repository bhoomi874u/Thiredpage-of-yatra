Historical Places

Kangra Fort

One of India’s oldest forts with panoramic Kangra Valley views.

Naggar Castle

15th-century stone & wood castle, now a heritage hotel with art gallery nearby.

Bhuri Singh Museum

Chamba museum for pahari paintings, coins & royal artefacts.

Tabo Monastery

“Ajanta of Himalayas” — ancient monastery with murals in Spiti.

Arki / Kuthar Fort

Hill forts with murals and gardens — small heritage sites worth visiting.

Famous Temples

Jwalamukhi Temple

Temple of the eternal flame — major Shakti peeth in Kangra.

Chintpurni Temple

One of Himachal’s Shaktipeeths in Una district; devotional hub.

Hadimba Devi Temple

Sacred wooden temple set in dense deodar forests near Manali.

Chamunda Devi Temple

Hill-top shrine near Dharamshala with panoramic views and devotion.

Bajreshwari / Jwala (Kangra)

Regional shrines dedicated to Shakti traditions in Kangra region.

Trekking Spots

Triund Trek

Popular easy trek from McLeodganj with Dhauladhar views; overnight camping possible.

Hampta Pass Trek

Classic Manali trek connecting lush valleys to stark Spiti landscapes.

Pin Parvati Pass

Challenging high-altitude trek linking Parvati valley to Pin valley (Spiti).

Beas Kund Trek

Trek to the source of the Beas river with alpine scenery; moderate difficulty.

Bhrigu Lake Trek

High-altitude lake trek near Manali; popular for alpine meadows & views.

Rivers & Water

Beas River

White-water rafting stretch near Kullu/Manali & riverside camping.

Chenab River

Flows through Lahaul & Spiti; dramatic gorges and high mountain scenery.

Parvati River

Beautiful river near Kasol, hamlets & backpacker culture on its banks.
