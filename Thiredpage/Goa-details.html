Let's Explore Goa

Goa is India's smallest state, located on the west coast and known for its beaches, Portuguese colonial history, and tourism. It has a tropical climate, a capital in Panaji, and borders Maharashtra and Karnataka, with the Arabian Sea to the west. The state's economy includes mining, agriculture, and tourism, with significant infrastructure and high quality of life metrics.

Location:

Situated on the western coast of India along the Arabian Sea, in the Konkan region.

Border:

North by Maharashtra, and east and south by Karnataka.

Area:

3,702 square kilometers (1,429 sq mi).

Coastline

Approximately 131 km (81 miles) of coastline with many beaches

Rivers:

Major rivers include Mandovi and Zuari.
